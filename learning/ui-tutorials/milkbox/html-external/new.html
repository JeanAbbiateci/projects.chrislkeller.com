2010 Governor's Race

Scott Walker's celebrated his 43rd birthday on Nov. 2, 2010 by beating back his Democratic challenger, Milwaukee Mayor Tom Barrett, and being elected as Wisconsin's 45th governor.

Walker earned 1,128,941 votes to Barrett's 1,004,303.
