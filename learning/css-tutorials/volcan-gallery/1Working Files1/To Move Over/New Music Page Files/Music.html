UMBRA AND THE VOLCAN SIEGE

In 2008, Jim Licka formed a sextet of Chicago musicians. They got heavy into Experimental and Improvisational Warfare, but it was from Jim's compositions that they produced "The Beginning of the End," the first album by Umbra And The Volcan Siege.


Umbra And The Volcan Siege is JIM LICKA on mellotron, Moog and guitar; CURTIS EVANS on guitar; DARREN SHEPPERD on drums; DAVE BOWERS on guitar; MIKE DOBERVICH on bass and DAVE GALLAGHER on guitar.


Umbra And The Volcan Seige will release "The Beginning of the End" on July 30, 2009 at The Kinetic Playground, 1113 W Lawrence Ave. in Chicago, and on Aug. 15, 2009 at their home base -- The Volcan Gallery -- in the Ravenswood neighborhood.

CURTIS EVANS

Curtis Evans was born 21 october, 1979 in Elgin, Illinois. He picked up a guitar and wrote his first song at age 15. He played in the rock group The Void from 1997 to 2000 with current members of acclaimed Chicago outfit, inchWORM. In 2004 he formed Our Friends Electric with proffesional online poker player Amos Lieberman and since 2006 has been performing throughout Chicago with various backing members. He is influenced first and foremost by The Beatles, later by Sparklehorse and Townes van Zandt. He enjoys long walks on the beach and dangerous amounts of alcohol and drugs.
