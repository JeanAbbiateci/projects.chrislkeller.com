ABOUT THE GALLERY

Volcan Gallery showcases an exclusive range of hand-made light designs from Ghent, Belgium; as well as premiere paintings, jewelry and live music from local artists.


The organically shaped metal chandeliers with hand blown glass bulbs will add a unique ambiance to your home or business.


Aside from the assortment of chandeliers, we also provide custom made designs according to your personal wishes. Once a month Volcan Gallery hosts live music from aspiring local Chicago musicians and works by local Chicago artists.


We are also available for private parties, for more information or to make an appointment, please contact Volcan Gallery at 773 880 8075
