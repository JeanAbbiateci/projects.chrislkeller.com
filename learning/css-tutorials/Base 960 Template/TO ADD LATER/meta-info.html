If motorists drove congestion-free through Chicago life as a commuter would be a dream. But instead we deal with the Circle Interchange, the Eisenhower Expressway and the Kennedy Expressway. Horrible traffic - and horrible drivers - are a way of life for Chicago commuters. That's where the ChiTown Traffic Tattler comes in, providing construction news and commuter information and hopefully some laughs along thw way. Let's call it group therapy for your commute.
